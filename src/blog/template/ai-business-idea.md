The utilization of artificial intelligence (AI) is no longer just a trend, but rather a fundamental infrastructure for modern business efficiency. Building SaaS (Software as a Service) products that integrate AI has tremendous scalability potential.

Here are a few business architectures I am currently researching:

1. **B2B Financial Report Automation**
   Using LLMs to extract and standardize data from thousands of irregular invoices or receipts into an audit-ready format.

2. **Domain-Specific Coding Assistant**
   Unlike general coding assistants, this AI is trained on an enterprise's internal _codebase_ repositories so it understands their internal architecture and security standards.

The key to these ideas is focusing on solving specific, _high-ticket pain points_ for companies, rather than just wrapping a ChatGPT _prompt_ into a regular application.
