AI-Based Business Ideas with Massive Scale Potential

The utilization of artificial intelligence (AI) is no longer just a trend, but rather a fundamental infrastructure for modern business efficiency. Building SaaS (Software as a Service) products that integrate AI has tremendous scalability potential.

Here are a few business architectures I am currently researching:

  1. B2B Financial Report Automation Using LLMs to extract and standardize data from thousands of irregular invoices or receipts into an audit-ready format.

  2. Domain-Specific Coding Assistant Unlike general coding assistants, this AI is trained on an enterprise’s internal codebase repositories so it understands their internal architecture and security standards.

The key to these ideas is focusing on solving specific, high-ticket pain points for companies, rather than just wrapping a ChatGPT prompt into a regular application.
